About the Dashboard

The Decision Support System (DSS) Dashboard is an innovative platform developed to streamline urban planning and decision-making in Sahiwal. It provides real-time data visualization and actionable insights, helping stakeholders effectively manage administrative boundaries, land use, proposed zones, and urban development proposals.

This user-friendly interface fosters collaboration among urban planners, government officials, and the public to create a sustainable, inclusive, and resilient city.

About PICIIP

The Punjab Intermediate Cities Improvement Investment Program (PICIIP) is committed to transforming intermediate cities into modern urban centers. By improving municipal infrastructure, services, and governance, PICIIP ensures a better quality of life for residents. Our focus is on sustainable development, climate resilience, and smart urban growth.
